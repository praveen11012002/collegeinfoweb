Green Club


The relationship between JMC and nature is a long and enduring one, something that students and staff of the college are aware of. The buildings of this educational institution stand on the solid rock of the Aravalli range, a topographical feature that the Congregation has consciously chosen to preserve and protect. JMC is very often recognized as the college with huge rocks jutting from the concrete giving it a completely natural and organic facade. Since 1968, the college has always had sustainable initiatives at the core of all activities. 11.88% of the 14.1acre lush campus in the Diplomatic Enclave has been built up. The rest of the campus can be categorized as playgrounds, lawns and gardens. A huge area is left in its natural form and acts as a natural habitat for biodiversity and a large variety of species of grasses, herbs, shrubs and trees.

Tamil Mandram


Tamil Mandram was started in 2016. Tamil is one of the longest-surviving classical languages in the world. The aim of this club is to encourage student’s active engagement in Tamil and to promote traditional culture among them. Tamil Mandram conducts competitions like elocution, debate, poetry, and creative writing.

  • To foster a fondness for Tamil and advance the language proficiency
  • To motivate the students in the active learning process of Tamil language.
  • To encourage them to participate in various interpersonal activities
  • To instill in them an appreciation for Tamil history and culture

Sports


It is our objective to offer the best possible training and coaching in various sports and games to all the students. We have excellent infrastructure facilities for providing training both indoor and outdoor sports and games. All the students are permitted to participate in all sports events organized outside throughout the year. Meritorious sports achievers are recognized aptly by the institution. During admission into engineering studies, meritorious sports students are offered 100% scholarship for perusing their studies without financial burden. Students are continually motivated to participate in Anna university tournaments & Open tournaments. All these efforts are made for making the students to be healthy and mould them as the best citizens of the nation.

Rotaract Club


The Rotaract Club of Akshaya College of Engineering and Technology is a college based club, sponsored by the Rotary Coimbatore Heritage and belongs to R.I. Dist. 3201. It was started on 02nd March 2015. 375 Rotaractors are enrolled in the club. We proudly say that our Club is having the biggest number of Rotaractors in the Rotary International District 3201, India. The Motto of the club is “Actions, not in Words."
